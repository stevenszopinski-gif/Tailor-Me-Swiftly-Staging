Getting Started

How to create your first tailored resume

  1. Upload your resume — Go to the app and drag & drop your resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT) onto the upload zone, or click to browse.
  2. Add the job description — Paste a job URL (Greenhouse, Lever, Workday supported) or paste the job description text directly.
  3. Generate — Click "Tailor My Resume" and our AI will analyze the match, rewrite your resume for ATS optimization, generate a cover letter, prepare interview questions, and draft an intro email.
  4. Review & download — View your results on the results page. Download as PDF or copy the HTML.

What is the ATS Match Score?

Your ATS Match Score (0-100%) measures how well your resume aligns with the target job description. It considers:

  • Keyword coverage — Do your skills, tools, and qualifications match what the employer asked for?
  • Experience alignment — Does your work history reflect the required responsibilities?
  • Missing keywords — Skills you're missing are flagged so you know what to add.

Aim for 75%+ for best results. Scores below 50% mean significant mismatches.

What file formats are supported?

We accept three resume formats:

  • PDF — Most common. We use PDF.js to extract text from all pages.
  • DOCX — Microsoft Word format. Parsed with Mammoth.js.
  • TXT — Plain text files.

For best results, make sure your PDF is text-based (not a scanned image). If text extraction fails, try saving your resume as DOCX first.

Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in, you land on the Dashboard — your job search command center:

  • Quick Stats — See total applications, applied, interviewing, and offers at a glance.
  • Mini Kanban — Visual pipeline of all your applications by status. Click any card to open its detail modal in the full tracker.
  • New Application — Click to start a new resume tailoring session.
  • Full Tracker — Opens the complete drag-and-drop Kanban board.
  • Career Command Center — Access standalone tools like Career Pivot, Skills Tracker, and Auto-App Agent.
Resume & Cover Letter

How to download your tailored resume

On the Results page after generation:

  1. Click the "Download PDF" button above your resume preview.
  2. Your browser will generate a clean, ATS-friendly PDF using html2pdf.
  3. You can also copy the HTML to paste into Google Docs or another editor.

All generated resumes are automatically saved to your account and accessible from the Dashboard or Application Tracker.

Generating a cover letter

A cover letter is automatically generated as part of the tailoring process. You can also:

  • Navigate to the Cover Letter tool from your results page for a standalone version.
  • Choose between different tones (Professional, Warm, Bold) for your thank-you emails.
  • Use the Pain Letter tool to write a company-specific pain-point letter instead of a traditional cover letter.

Understanding missing keywords

After tailoring, you'll see a list of missing keywords — skills or qualifications the job description mentions that aren't in your resume.

  • Keywords you actually have can be selected via the AI Co-Pilot — the AI will weave them into your bullet points.
  • Keywords you don't have represent genuine skill gaps. Consider upskilling or addressing them in your cover letter.
  • Use the Skills Tracker tool to see which skills are rising or decaying in your industry.

Account & Billing

Upgrading to Premium

TailorMeSwiftly Premium is $9.99/month and unlocks all 20+ AI career tools with unlimited generations.

  1. Click any locked tool or the "Upgrade" button in the app.
  2. Complete checkout via Stripe (secure, PCI-compliant).
  3. Your account is upgraded instantly — no page refresh needed.

Manage your subscription, update payment methods, or cancel anytime from the Account page.

Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel anytime with no fees:

  1. Go to Account from the avatar dropdown.
  2. Click "Manage Subscription" to open the Stripe customer portal.
  3. Click "Cancel plan" in the portal.

After cancellation, you retain premium access until the end of your billing period. Your saved applications and data are never deleted.

Resetting your password

If you signed up with email and forgot your password:

  1. Go to the Login page.
  2. Click "Forgot password?" below the login form.
  3. Enter your email address and click "Send Reset Link".
  4. Check your inbox for the reset email and follow the link.

If you signed up with Google, there's no separate password — just click "Log In with Google".

Deleting your account

To permanently delete your account and all data:

  1. Go to Account.
  2. Scroll to the Danger Zone section.
  3. Click "Delete My Account" and confirm.

This permanently removes your profile, all saved applications, and cancels any active Stripe subscription. This action cannot be undone.

Application Tracker

Using the Kanban board

The Application Tracker is a full drag-and-drop Kanban board with five columns:

  • Tailored — Resume generated but not yet applied.
  • Applied — You've submitted the application.
  • Interviewing — You have an interview scheduled. Extra fields appear for interview date, contacts, and follow-ups.
  • Offer — You received an offer.
  • Rejected — Application was rejected.

Drag cards between columns to update their status. On mobile, use the dropdown selector on each card instead.

Adding notes to applications

Click any card on the Kanban board to open the detail modal:

  • Write free-form notes (interview dates, contact names, follow-up reminders).
  • Set a reminder date — overdue reminders appear as amber banners at the top of the tracker.
  • For cards in the Interviewing column, fill in structured fields for interview date, contacts, and follow-ups.
  • Click "Go to Application" to view the full generated resume and tools for that application.
Troubleshooting

Page appears blank or won't load

If a page appears blank or stuck on a loading spinner:

  1. Hard refresh — Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac).
  2. Clear cache — Cached CSS or JS files may be outdated. Clear your browser cache or try incognito mode.
  3. Check your connection — A stable internet connection is required for AI generation and data loading.
  4. Try a different browser — We support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

If the issue persists, check the Release Notes for known issues or contact support.

Resume text extraction fails

If your resume uploads but shows "No text found":

  • Your PDF may be a scanned image rather than text-based. Open it in a PDF editor and try "Save as Text" or re-export from Word.
  • Password-protected PDFs cannot be parsed. Remove the password first.
  • Try saving your resume as DOCX and re-uploading — DOCX parsing is more reliable for complex layouts.
  • As a last resort, paste your resume text directly into the text area using the "Paste text instead" option.

Job URL import not working

Not all job URLs can be automatically fetched. If URL import fails:

  • Some sites (LinkedIn, Indeed) block automated fetching. Copy and paste the job description text manually instead.
  • Supported job board formats include Greenhouse, Lever, and Workday (with JSON-LD structured data).
  • Make sure the URL is a direct link to the job posting, not a search results page.
  • If the URL works but extracts the wrong text, paste the JD manually for best results.

Can't log in or sign up

Common login issues and fixes:

  • Google login popup blocked — Allow popups for tailormeswiftly.com in your browser settings.
  • "Authentication not ready" — Wait a moment and try again. If it persists, hard refresh the page.
  • Email confirmation required — If you signed up with email, check your inbox (and spam folder) for the confirmation link. You must verify your email before logging in.
  • Forgot password — Click "Forgot password?" on the login page to receive a reset link.
