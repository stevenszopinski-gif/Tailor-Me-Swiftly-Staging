Release Notes & Updates

Stay up to date with the latest features, enhancements, and bug fixes.

v3.2.0 — Voice, Reliability & Design

v3.2.0 • Current
  • ElevenLabs TTS: Mock interview questions are now read aloud using a premium ElevenLabs AI voice, with browser TTS as a seamless fallback.
  • Reliable Job URL Fetching: Pasting a job listing URL now fetches through our own server, replacing the old third-party proxies that frequently failed.
  • Resume Quick Presets: Redesigned preset buttons with color-coded accent dots. Switching presets now correctly updates the accent color picker and all related styles.
  • Use Last Resume: Returning users can instantly load their previously uploaded resume on Step 1 instead of re-uploading.
  • Results Page Refresh: Updated header and background to match the main app’s look and feel with gradient background effects.
  • Improved Skill Chips: Missing skills on the Gaps step are now much easier to read with higher-contrast colors in both light and dark modes.
  • Personalized Greeting: A “Hi, [Name]” greeting now appears under your avatar across all pages.

v3.1.0 — UX Polish & Improvements

v3.1.0
  • 10 Mock Interview Questions: Increased from 8 to 10 tailored interview questions for more comprehensive preparation.
  • Improved Text-to-Speech: More natural voice delivery with optimized rate and pitch for better interview practice.
  • Dark Resume Preset: New high-contrast "Dark" theme with black background and white text for maximum readability.
  • Results Grid Layout: All 4 outputs (resume, cover letter, mock interview, introduction email) now display in a single row for better overview.
  • Updated Homepage: Moved TailorMeSwiftly.com branding above the animation, cleaner header design.
  • Removed LinkedIn Optimization: Streamlined feature set to focus on core strengths.
  • Enhanced Loading Experience: Improved funny quotes display and removed unnecessary time estimates.
  • Messaging Refresh: Updated homepage copy to highlight lightning-fast results.

v3.0.0 — Major Update

v3.0.0
  • Gaps Assistant: Renamed and refined the skill gap analysis step for a clearer, more focused workflow.
  • Mock Interview Mode: Hear questions read aloud with text-to-speech. Practice one question at a time with Next/Previous navigation and AI-powered feedback on your recorded answers.
  • On-Demand Generation: Interview prep and introduction email drafts now generate only when you click their tab, making the initial resume+cover letter tailoring significantly faster.
  • Application Tracker Fix: Kanban board now loads your tailored resumes from the cloud. Drag-and-drop status updates persist to your account.
  • Resume Versions: All tailored resumes are saved with version names. View and reload any past version from the sidebar.
  • Saved Jobs: Bookmark job descriptions in Step 2 and tailor resumes against them later.
  • Career Profile: Store a master career history in Preferences and use it as a base for all future tailoring.
  • Loading Redesign: Full-screen centered loading overlay with blurred background during generation.
  • Preferences Dropdown: Preferences and sign-out now accessible from a clean dropdown on your profile avatar.
  • Generation Timeout: API calls now have timeouts with retry buttons to prevent stuck generation states.
