Release Notes & Updates

Stay up to date with the latest features, enhancements, and bug fixes.

v3.4.0 — Kanban Tracker, Notes & Company Intel

Current
  • Kanban Application Tracker: Your applications now live on a full drag-and-drop Kanban board with five columns—Tailored, Applied, Interviewing, Offer, and Rejected.
  • Click-to-Open Cards: Click any Kanban card to open a detail modal with notes, a “Go to Application” button, and Save/Cancel controls.
  • Application Notes: Add free-form notes to any application—interview dates, contact names, follow-up reminders.
  • Interview Details: Cards in the Interviewing column surface structured fields for interview date, contacts, and follow-ups.
  • Company Intel: The Introduction Email page now auto-fetches recent news and insights about your target company.
  • Light Theme Default: The app now defaults to light mode for new users.
  • UI Polish: Opaque dropdown menus, wider navigation labels, and improved auth session handling.

v3.3.0 — Past Applications & History

v3.3.0
  • Past Applications: All your generated resumes, cover letters, interview Q&A, and outreach emails are now automatically saved to your account.
  • One-Click Restore: Instantly reload any previous application’s outputs without re-generating.
  • Incremental Saves: When you generate a cover letter, interview questions, or outreach email on-demand, the save updates automatically.
  • History Navigation: Quick links to Past Applications are now available throughout the app.

v3.2.0 — Voice, Reliability & Design

v3.2.0
  • ElevenLabs TTS: Mock interview questions are now read aloud using a premium ElevenLabs AI voice, with browser TTS as a fallback.
  • Reliable Job URL Fetching: Pasting a job listing URL now fetches through our own server, replacing old third-party proxies.
  • Resume Quick Presets: Redesigned preset buttons with color-coded accent dots.
  • Use Last Resume: Returning users can instantly load their previously uploaded resume.
  • Results Page Refresh: Updated header and background to match the main app’s look and feel.
  • Improved Skill Chips: Missing skills now have higher-contrast colors in both themes.
  • Personalized Greeting: A “Hi, [Name]” greeting now appears under your avatar.
